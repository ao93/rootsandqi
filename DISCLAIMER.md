# Disclaimer & Compliance Notice

## Medical Disclaimer

RootsAndQi is an educational and portfolio project. It is **not** a medical device,
clinical decision support tool, or substitute for professional medical advice,
diagnosis, or treatment.

The syndrome classifications, herb recommendations, and wellness insights generated
by this application are based on traditional wellness frameworks — specifically
Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) and Indigenous herbal traditions — and are
provided for informational and educational purposes only.

**Always consult a qualified healthcare provider** before making any health-related
decisions, starting any herbal regimen, or discontinuing any prescribed treatment.

---

## Traditional Knowledge Acknowledgment

This project draws on Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) and Indigenous herbal
traditions from multiple cultures. These knowledge systems represent centuries of
accumulated wisdom developed by communities around the world.

We acknowledge that:

- Traditional healing knowledge belongs to the communities that developed it
- This project uses these traditions for educational exploration only
- No claim of authenticity, completeness, or cultural authority is made
- Commercial use of this application would require appropriate consultation
  with and compensation to knowledge-holding communities

---

## AI-Generated Content

The syndrome classifications and herb recommendations in this application are
generated by a large language model (Anthropic Claude) via a LangChain pipeline.
AI-generated health information:

- May be incomplete, inaccurate, or inappropriate for individual circumstances
- Should never be acted upon without professional medical consultation
- Does not account for individual health history, medications, or contraindications
- Is not reviewed or approved by any medical or regulatory authority

---

## Herb Safety

Herbal remedies can interact with medications, cause allergic reactions, and be
contraindicated in certain conditions including pregnancy, breastfeeding, and
chronic illness. **Never use herbs medicinally without consulting a qualified
practitioner.**

---

## Data Privacy

This application does not store, log, or transmit user-submitted symptom data
beyond what is necessary to generate a response. MLflow experiment tracking logs
aggregate metrics (confidence scores, herb counts) but does not log the content
of individual symptom submissions in production.

---

## Regulatory Status

This application has not been reviewed or approved by the FDA, EMA, or any other
regulatory authority. It does not meet the definition of a medical device under
any current regulatory framework and is not intended to do so.

## Portfolio & Educational Use

This project was built as a software engineering portfolio piece to demonstrate
DevOps, MLOps, and AI integration skills. The subject matter (TCM + Indigenous
herbal medicine) was chosen because of the developer's personal background in
health science and interest in integrative wellness traditions — not to provide
actual medical services.
